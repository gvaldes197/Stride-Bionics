About Stride Bionics

The Strider is a powered lower-limb exoskeleton designed specifically for stroke rehabilitation. Unlike traditional rehab robots that require a therapist or caregiver, Strider is being designed for safe, independent use at home or in the community.

Our system uses adaptive feedback control and real-time safety monitoring to help users walk again with confidence - no upper limb power, no bulky cables, no clinical tethering.

This device is not FDA cleared and is not available for sale or clinical use.

Pricing Models We Are Testing

Model 1: Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS)

A monthly subscription that includes the Strider hardware, software updates, and remote support. Ideal for clinics or short-term rehab programs.

Model 2: Direct Purchase

One-time purchase of the Strider with optional extended warranty and training packages. Suitable for institutions and long-term users.

Model 3: Community Lease-to-Own

Users lease the device for 6 to 12 months with the option to apply payments toward a final purchase. Designed for at-home use post-discharge.

Pricing models are conceptual only. The device cannot be sold, leased, or distributed until FDA clearance is granted.
